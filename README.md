# Churn_Analysis-EDA-
The "Support" Safety Net: There is a strong correlation between a lack of technical add-ons and customer departure. Customers without Tech Support, Online Security, or Online Backup are significantly more likely to churn, as these services act as "loyalty anchors".Payment Method Risks: The Electronic Check payment method is a major "red flag," showing the highest volume of churned customers.Payment Loyalty: In contrast, customers using Automatic Payments (Credit Card or Bank Transfer) exhibit the highest levels of loyalty.Contractual Stability: Customers on Month-to-month contracts are more prone to leaving than those on long-term (one or two-year) commitments.Customer Longevity: The average tenure for customers in this dataset is approximately 32.37 months.Billing Patterns: The mean monthly charge is $64.76, with total charges per customer averaging $2,279.73.Senior Citizen Participation: Senior citizens represent 16.21% of the total customer base, accounting for 1,142 out of the 7,043 records.
